Frequently asked questions

Why can't I compress more than 10 photos today?

The free version of Compressly includes up to 10 compressions per day. If you need more, upgrade to Compressly Premium to unlock unlimited compressions and batch compression. Your daily limit resets automatically each day.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Compressly, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same App Store or Google Play account you used for the original purchase. If Premium still doesn't unlock, contact us and we'll help.

Are my photos uploaded?

No. All compression happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere, and Compressly requires no account and uses no cloud storage.

Does Compressly work offline?

Yes. Because everything is processed on your device, Compressly works fully offline — compress photos anywhere, no internet connection required.
