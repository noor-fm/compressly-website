Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Compressly ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Compressly mobile application (the "App") and this website. We built Compressly with privacy as a core principle: your photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to us or anyone else.

1. Your Photos Never Leave Your Device

All photo compression happens locally, on your device. Compressly:

  • Never uploads your photos to any server.
  • Never stores your photos in any cloud service.
  • Never analyzes the content of your photos for any purpose other than compression, which happens entirely on your device.

The App requests access to your photo library solely so that you can select photos to compress and save the compressed results. This access is controlled by your device's operating system, and you can revoke it at any time in your device settings.

2. Information We Collect

Compressly does not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal profile information in order for you to use the App.

The following limited categories of information may be processed:

  • Advertising data (free version): The free version of the App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), coarse location derived from IP address, and app interaction data to serve and measure ads. See Section 4 below.
  • Purchase information: If you purchase Compressly Premium, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We receive anonymized purchase receipts to unlock Premium features; we never receive your payment card details.
  • Diagnostics: The app stores (Apple and Google) may provide us with aggregated, anonymized crash reports and usage statistics to help us improve the App.

We do not sell any information, and we do not use your information for any purpose other than operating and improving the App.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide the App's features, including unlocking Premium after a purchase.
  • To display advertising in the free version of the App.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability and performance.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Compressly uses Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display ads. AdMob may use device identifiers and related data to serve ads, limit how often you see an ad, and measure ad performance. Depending on your region and device settings, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

You can limit ad personalization through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising.
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete or reset your advertising ID.

For more information about how Google uses data, please see Google's Privacy & Terms and the AdMob privacy documentation.

Compressly Premium removes ads; no advertising data is collected in the Premium version.

5. In-App Purchases

Compressly offers an optional Premium upgrade via in-app purchase. Payments are handled entirely by the App Store (Apple) or Google Play (Google) using your existing store account. We do not collect, see, or store your payment details. Purchase receipts are validated only to unlock Premium features and to restore your purchase on a new device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising in the free version) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store (app distribution and in-app purchases on iOS) — Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Play (app distribution and in-app purchases on Android) — Google Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review these policies. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the practices of these third parties.

7. Data Retention

Because your photos never leave your device, we have no photo data to retain. Any data processed by third-party services (such as advertising identifiers) is retained according to those services' own policies. Support emails you send us are kept only as long as needed to resolve your request.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the GDPR in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom, or the CCPA/CPRA in California), you may have rights regarding personal information, including the right to:

  • Access the personal information held about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 4).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Since Compressly itself collects essentially no personal information, most of these rights are best exercised through your device settings and the third-party services listed above. For anything else, contact us using the details in Section 11 and we will do our best to help.

9. Children's Privacy

Compressly is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in the App or in legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, provide a more prominent notice. Your continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how Compressly handles data, please contact us at:
